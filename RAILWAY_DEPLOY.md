# Deploy to Railway - Step by Step

Your code is already on GitHub: https://github.com/mydaniel112-hub/golf-Claude

## Steps (No Terminal Commands Needed!):

### 1. Go to Railway
Visit: https://railway.app

### 2. Sign Up
- Click "Start a New Project"
- Sign up with **GitHub** (use your GitHub account)
- Authorize Railway to access your repos

### 3. Create New Project
- Click **"New Project"**
- Select **"Deploy from GitHub repo"**
- Find and select: **`golf-Claude`**
- Click it

### 4. Railway Auto-Deploys!
- Railway automatically:
  - Detects it's a Python project
  - Installs dependencies from `requirements.txt`
  - Starts the server
  - Gives you a live URL!

### 5. Get Your URL
- Railway will show you a URL like: `https://golf-claude-production.up.railway.app`
- Click it to see your app!

### 6. Access from iPhone
- Open Safari on iPhone
- Go to your Railway URL
- Allow camera access
- Press "Start" to begin tracking!

---

## That's It!

No terminal commands needed - it's all done through the Railway website!

Your app will be live and accessible from anywhere in the world.
